Key Projects

Retail Demand Forecasting (Time Series + MLOps)

Built an end-to-end retail demand forecasting pipeline using SARIMAX and Prophet, evaluated with walk-forward cross-validation and strong baselines (naive, seasonal naive). Implemented robust metrics (sMAPE, WAPE, MASE), target transformations, and experiment tracking with MLflow. Deployed a lightweight FastAPI service for generating forecasts.

Key insight: Seasonal naive outperformed complex models, showing the importance of strong baselines in real-world forecasting. Best model achieved 17.34% sMAPE on rolling validation.
